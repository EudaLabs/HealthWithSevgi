### User Testing Protocol

Each group must conduct a structured usability test. The participant must be a non-computer-science person with university-level education. The test must be completed independently (no coaching during the task).

| <br> Task <br> | <br> What the Participant Must Do <br> | <br> Success Criterion <br> | <br> Time Limit <br> |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| <br> T1 <br> | <br> Open the tool, locate the domain pill bar, and switch between two different medical specialties. <br> | <br> Clicks correct pills; Step 1 content updates for each; no errors. <br> | <br> 90 sec <br> |
| <br> T2 <br> | <br> Upload the provided sample CSV file and open the Column Mapper. <br> | <br> CSV accepted and Column Mapper opens without error. <br> | <br> 3 min <br> |
| <br> T3 <br> | <br> Complete Column Mapper validation and proceed to Step 3. <br> | <br> Clicks Save; green banner appears; navigates to Step 3. <br> | <br> 2 min <br> |
| <br> T4 <br> | <br> Apply preparation settings in Step 3 and move to Step 4. <br> | <br> Clicks Apply; green success banner appears; opens Step 4. <br> | <br> 3 min <br> |
| <br> T5 <br> | <br> Train a KNN model and find the Sensitivity score in Step 5. <br> | <br> Correctly identifies the Sensitivity value and its colour. <br> | <br> 3 min <br> |
| <br> T6 <br> | <br> Find which patient measurement had the most influence on predictions (Step 6). <br> | <br> Names the top feature in the importance chart. <br> | <br> 2 min <br> |
| <br> T7 <br> | <br> Download the Summary Certificate. <br> | <br> PDF downloads successfully. <br> | <br> 1 min <br> |
